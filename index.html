Hi! My name is Silvio

Starting in September 2025, I will be a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Departement of Mathematics of MIT, working in the group Nanostructures and Computation lead by Prof. S. G. Johnson.

I defended my PhD in April 2025 after working at the Department of Mathematics of ETH Zurich working under the supervision of Prof. H. Ammari.

I research photonic and phononic wave propagation in complex media. I am mostly interested in non-Hermitian systems with a geometric component.
